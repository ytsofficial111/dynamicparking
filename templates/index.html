What We Do & Why We Do

The world's population is growing, and cities are becoming more crowded, resulting in an increase in the number of automobiles on the road. The management of car parking is one of the primary difficulties in cities.

As a result, the goal of this project is to create and construct a smart parking system by booking available slots employing mobile application technology.

Here, multiple cameras and artificial intelligence technique, which recognizes the vehicle number using embedded camera, tracks which parking space the vehicle is parked in, and updates parking space information.
